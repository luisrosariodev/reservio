FAQ
Quick answers before you book.

Is Reserv.io a marketplace?
No. Reserv.io is a controlled SaaS — only registered trainers appear, so clients book with verified providers.
How does ATH Móvil work?
After booking, you’ll see a QR code and instructions. The trainer manually confirms once payment is received.
Can I reserve multiple sessions?
Yes. Select multiple time slots across multiple days, then complete one checkout.
